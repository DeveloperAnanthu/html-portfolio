My top 3 Movies for the years 2022 & 2023

1) KGF 2

The film speaks of the pain of a son who gave his mother a word that he'll be the richest person on the earth one day.
His mother didn't had sufficient cash to pay the hospital expenses to take good treatment,
hence she decided to take all the pains in her sholder and raise her son.
When his mother passed away due to illness the society treated him the worse.
Then the story will show you his Life of Rockey, how became the don of Mumbai.

The cast for the movie were as follows

  • Yash as Raja Krishnappa Bairya "Rocky"
  • Anmol Vijay as young Rocky[22]
  • Sanjay Dutt as Adheera, Suryavardhan's brother
  • Raveena Tandon as Ramika Sen, Prime Minister of India
  • Srinidhi Shetty as Reena Desai, Rocky's girlfriend and wife
  • Achyuth Kumar as Guru Pandian, DYSS Party head
  • Prakash Raj as Vijayendra Ingalagi, Anand Ingalagi's son[23]
  • Vasishta N. Simha as Kamal
